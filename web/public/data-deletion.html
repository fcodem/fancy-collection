Fancy Collection by Renu Agarwal

This page explains how to request deletion of personal data associated with Fancy Collection by Renu Agarwal, including data processed with our Meta / WhatsApp Business integration.

How to request deletion

  1. WhatsApp or call 8077843874 or 8630834711.
  2. Write clearly: “Please delete my personal data”.
  3. Include your phone/WhatsApp number, full name, and booking reference if known.
  4. We will verify the request and delete or anonymise eligible records, including related WhatsApp conversation history we store, where we are not legally required to retain it.

What we may retain

We may keep limited information for tax, accounting, fraud prevention, active rentals, or legal disputes.

Meta / Facebook App note

Our Meta App is used for WhatsApp Business messaging. We do not create Facebook Login customer accounts. Deletion requests are handled manually through the contact channels above.
